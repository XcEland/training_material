# Executive Panel Question Bank

Use these questions during capstone presentations.

## ROI and KPIs

1. What assumptions drive your ROI calculation?
2. Which assumption is most likely to be wrong?
3. What KPI will prove the initiative is working after 90 days?
4. What benefit is not captured in the financial ROI?

## Security and Compliance

1. How does your design enforce least privilege?
2. How do you prevent SQL injection?
3. Where are credentials stored?
4. What audit evidence can be produced for an external reviewer?

## Deployment and Operations

1. What is your rollback plan?
2. What happens if the automated job fails during month-end?
3. Who owns monitoring after go-live?
4. How will users be trained and supported?

## Strategy

1. Why is this the right first initiative?
2. What should be done in the next 6-18 months?
3. Which emerging technology would you evaluate, and why?
4. What would make leadership stop or delay this initiative?
